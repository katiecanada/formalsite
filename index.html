Q. What is included in the price of your ticket?

Two drink tickets and hors d'oeuvres, and of course great music, great venue, great friends! We’ll also have lots of fun activities during the night including photos and a dance floor.

Q. What is the attire for Senior Formal?

This is a formal event. Women will most likely arrive in both short and long formal dresses, and men will most likely wear suits but this will vary as well.

Q. Are dates expected?

Dates are not expected. Most people will attend formal in a group of friends, but of course some people will have dates. Feel free to ask a date to go with you, but it is by no means necessary or encouraged. Last year 1200 people attended senior formal, demonstrating that this event is about enjoying a special night with your friends and your class.

Tickets:
Formal tickets are $70 per person until 5/1, $75 until 5/15, and then $80 until 5/26.

All students purchasing tickets must be Stanford students; however, Stanford students may purchase tickets for non-Stanford guests. These guests must have a valid photo ID and must be 18 years or older.

A ticket includes two drink tickets and hors d'oeuvres, and of course great music, great venue, great friends! We’ll also have lots of fun activities during the night, including a dance floor and photos.

Transportation:
Round trip bus tickets are $20 per person. Buses will leave Tresidder at 9:15pm and return from the venue at 2am.
