Q. What is included in the price of your ticket?

Two drink tickets and hors d'oeuvres including sliders and cake pops, and of course great music, great venue, great friends! We’ll also have lots of fun activities during the night, including a photobooth, two dance floors, and live performances.

Q. What is the attire for Senior Formal?

This is a formal event. Women will most likely arrive in both short and long formal dresses, and men will most likely wear suits but this will vary as well.

Q. Are dates expected?

Dates are not expected. Most people will attend formal in a group of friends, but of course some people will have dates. Feel free to ask a date to go with you, but it is by no means necessary or encouraged. Last year 1200 people attended senior formal, demonstrating that this event is about enjoying a special night with your friends and your class.

Q. What type of music will there be?

Keep watching this space for DJ information! What we can say is that we’ll have a dance floor with top-40 music, and a social dance floor with swing and waltz.

Q.What are the details on transportation?

Round-trip bus tickets from Stanford are $20, and can be bought online at seniorformal.stanford.edu. Buses will leave at 9:15pm (see late bus information below) and will return around 2:45am. A free shuttle will be running from The W hotel and the Winery, starting at 9:45pm. Please note that getting taxis from Treasure Island can be difficult, so taking the shuttle is a great option. There’s tons of free parking available at the Winery. However, we encourage you to be safe and consider taking the the shuttle instead.

Q. I have something else to do that night? Will there be late buses leaving campus?

Yes! A limited number of late bus tickets will be available. To sign up for the late bus, buy a bus ticket online at seniorformal.stanford.edu and then email seniorformal2012@gmail.com with your name. This is a great option if you want to go to one of the shows on campus before Formal. For example:
The Stanford Shakespeare Company Presents
Romeo and Juliet
May 23-25 & 27 at 8pm
*Saturday, May 26 at 7pm
Oak Tree in the Engineering Quad

Q. I have more questions! Who should I contact?

You can email us at seniorformal2012@gmail.com.

Tickets:
Formal tickets are $70 per person until 5/1, $75 until 5/15, and then $80 until 5/26.

All students purchasing tickets must be Stanford students; however, Stanford students may purchase tickets for non-Stanford guests. These guests must have a valid photo ID and must be 18 years or older.

A ticket includes two drink tickets and hors d'oeuvres including sliders and cake pops, and of course great music, great venue, great friends! We’ll also have lots of fun activities during the night, including a photobooth, two dance floors, and live performances.
